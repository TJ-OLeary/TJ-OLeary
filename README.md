## Who I Am

10+ years in systems and network administration. I've built, maintained, and
broken infrastructure — and I've learned more from the breaking than the building.

The *why* and the *how* matter. Always. That's not a methodology — it's the
only honest way to operate. If you don't understand what's underneath, you
don't understand anything at all.

Linux is my daily driver — not by habit, but by conviction. It rewards curiosity,
punishes assumptions, and gives you the kind of low-level control that other
systems hide behind a friendly face. I/O redirection alone is worth the price
of admission.
